Volunteer with Us!
Join Us in Making Every Visit a Discovery!

Give back through creativity and working with children.

The Bay Area Discovery Museum is seeking responsible, reliable, and energetic individuals to join our Volunteer Program. 

Volunteers Have the Opportunity to: 

Inspire Others:

  • Welcome visitors and engage families throughout the museum.
  • Support programs by preparing materials behind the scenes.
  • Inspire curiosity and joyful learning at festivals and events.

Who Are We Looking For:

  • Volunteers 15 years or older.
  • Passionate about hands-on learning.
  • Interested in working with young children.

Types of Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Our flexible volunteer opportunities make it easy to get involved with just a 3-hour shift at a time. Choose from regular ongoing Discovery Zone support, seasonal opportunities, or one-off opportunities like supporting our festivals and special events. Volunteer when it works for you and make a meaningful impact in your community.

Make a difference in the lives of young children while having fun and connecting with your community. 

Volunteer Here!

We Provide Training

We equip you with the skills and resources needed to support our daily museum programs and engage with families.

We Value Inclusivity

We encourage those of all backgrounds to apply! We are a diverse community and representation matters!

Apply Here

FAQ

Important Information

Volunteers must be at least 15 years old. 

A background check is required for all regular volunteers over 15. 

The museum does not permit volunteering for individuals with offenses involving minors, serious drug or alcohol crimes, or acts of violence. 

The Bay Area Discovery Museum reserves the right to decline or discontinue volunteer participation at any time. 

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

We ask that volunteers have some experience working with children.

What is the time commitment to volunteer?

We ask for at least a 3-hour commitment. Find an opportunity that aligns with your interests and schedule.

How do I apply to be a Volunteer?

Prospective volunteers will fill out an application form. Click Apply Here! button to access form.

I want to bring a big group to volunteer!

We welcome both individuals and volunteer groups of up to six people, per day. To schedule a volunteer session, please contact volunteer@badm.org

If you are a Corporate Group interested in volunteering, please reach out to development@badm.org
